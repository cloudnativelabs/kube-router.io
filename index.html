Primary Features

IPVS/LVS Service Proxy

--run-service-proxy

Super fast in-kernel loadbalancing for Services. Drop-in kube-proxy replacement.

Pod Networking

--run-router

Direct routing with a modern BGP API. No external datastore or CNI plugins.

Network Policy Controller

--run-firewall

An efficient distributed firewall for your Pods.

Advanced BGP Capabilities

Integrates with your SDN or hardware network easily using Kubernetes resources for configuration.
